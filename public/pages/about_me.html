20/20/20 Timer: About the Creator

Hello, I'm Michael Gutierrez, amateur web designer and creator of this 20/20/20 timer. I am 26 years old and I am working towards earning my MS in Software Development from Maryville University in St. Louis, Missouri; I had previously earned my BA from the University of Texas at El Paso. This web application stemmed from a personal desire to monitor my own time spent working in front of screens. Hopefully, this site can inspire others to follow the 20/20/20 rule and help them engage in computer-based work in a safe and healthy manner.
